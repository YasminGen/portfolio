3D design

An interactive concept inspired by the visual world of Gentle Monster. Developed in Spline, the project explores a fictional pair of glasses that reveal a perfect world through an imagined future technology. The experience is designed as a guided spatial tour, inviting users to move through the environment and gradually uncover the idea behind the product. Third semester group project, 2025.
