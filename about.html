About me

I'm Yasmin Gendelevich, a graphic design and visual communication student based in Berlin, originally from Tel Aviv. I study at Berlin International University of Applied Sciences, where I work on different design projects as part of my program.

I enjoy working with typography, layout, and visual composition. I like trying different approaches, learning new tools, and building a solid skill set in design. Studying in Berlin keeps me surrounded by a lot of visual culture, which motivates me and supports the way I work.
